GoBabyGo

During my senior year of high school, I collaborated with three peers on a passion project aimed at repurposing a children's ride car into a joystick-controlled mobility device using Arduino Nano and Java programming. Our goal was to donate this innovative solution to a children's rehabilitation center, specifically targeting children too young for motorized wheelchairs. Through this endeavor, I strengthened my technical skills while learning the importance of empathy-driven design and community impact. This project reflects my commitment to using technology for social good and my dedication to collaborative problem-solving for the benefit of marginalized communities.

Vector Functions

This challenge not only deepened my understanding of vector mathematics and algorithmic problem-solving but also honed my proficiency in C programming.
